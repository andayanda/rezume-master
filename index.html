My Services

Stylist

Fashion stylist expert in e-commerce, content and editorial, specialized in creating attractive and consistent images with the brand. From product selection to composition and final editing.

Set Costumer & Tailor

Design and costume creation according to the production requirements; Costume fittings, on-site adjustments, dressing, alterations and maintenance of costumes. Coordination with the production team.

Quality Assurance

Factory inspection, review of cutting and production monitoring, sample control, taking measurements, meeting deadlines and solving problems.

Design

Sketching by hand and using computer-aided design (CAD) software, technical sheets, materials, pattern making and preparation of samples, fittings, and technical sheets; small-scale production, either through local tailoring workshops or in my workshop; project management and customer relations.

Multitasking

As founder and CEO of Kalypso, I have the sole responsibility of leading all facets of the business from conception to execution. My role covers a wide range of responsibilities, from product design to financial management and brand development. As the only employee, I am the driving force behind the brand's success and work tirelessly to make my vision a reality.
